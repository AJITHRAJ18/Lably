About Lab in English

Lab in English was created to make medical lab reports less intimidating and more understandable for everyday people.

Traditional lab reports are packed with numbers, abbreviations, and reference ranges that can be confusing or even frightening when viewed without context. Lab in English uses AI to turn those results into calm, plain-English explanations you can actually read and discuss with your doctor.

What we do

  • Help you understand the meaning of common biomarkers and lab panels.
  • Highlight which results are within, above, or below typical ranges.
  • Suggest thoughtful questions to bring to your next appointment.

Lab in English is not a replacement for medical advice. It is a companion tool to help you feel more prepared and less anxious when looking at your results.
