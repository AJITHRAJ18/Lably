Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Lab in English ("we", "us", or "our") helps you understand your medical lab reports in plain English. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you provide when you use our service.

Information we collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with your email via Supabase, we store your email address and a unique user ID.
  • Uploaded lab reports: When you upload a PDF lab report, we process it to extract text and generate explanations. We do not intend to use these PDFs for marketing purposes.
  • Billing information: Payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store full payment card details on our servers.
  • Usage information: We may collect basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited to operate and improve the service.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core service of translating your lab reports into plain English.
  • To manage your account, subscriptions, and report credits.
  • To maintain and improve the reliability, performance, and security of the site.
  • To communicate with you about important updates to the service.

How we handle uploaded lab reports

Uploaded PDFs are processed on our servers and by third-party AI providers (such as Anthropic or Google) to generate explanations. We aim to keep files only as long as necessary to provide the service and troubleshoot issues. You should avoid uploading reports that contain more information than is necessary.

Third-party services

  • Supabase for authentication and data storage.
  • Razorpay for payment processing.
  • AI providers such as Anthropic and Google for generating explanations.
  • Analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) for usage statistics.

Data security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • You can choose not to create an account and instead use the service in a limited, anonymous way if available.
